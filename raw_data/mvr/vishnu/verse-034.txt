  Sri Vishnu Sahasranama Stotram
      _20 PRADHAANA PURUSHESHWARAH

 Pradhaana is addressed as Prakriti by Saankhya religion and as Maaya by Advaita religion .
  
   PRADHAANA is Nature , the first  evolver or source of the material world  , the primary germ /source of the visible material universe . 
                      ------
 CREATION  -  Prakriti gave rise to Mahat tattwa which is undisturbed combo of three gunas Sattva Rajas and Tamas 

 Kshetrajna , Knower, merged with Prakriti at the time of creation 

 The basic Prakriti took forms as 5 goddesses Durga Lakshmi Saraswati Savitri and Radha according to Devee Bhagavatam .

 Mahat tattwa gave origin for 3 egos 1 Vaikaarika , 2 Taijasa and 3 Taamasa, the cause of 5 Bhootas ( elements ) , Indriyas ( senses ). 

  PURUSHA is Jeeva here.

   He is   the Knower of the Field , the Indwelling Antaraatma in Vedaanta 

 Prakriti and Purusha are matter and spirit , two basic factors essential for production of Prapancha ( the visible world )

 ( The Supreme Spirit divided itself into Prakriti and Purusha at the time of creation )
                     -----
  EESHWARA is the Supreme Ruler of the universe. He is Infinite and universal spirit, the cause and substance of creation .

 He , Vishnu , is Eeshwara supreme lord of both Pradhaana and Purusha meaning He rules over Maaya and Jeeva both ... Bubhukshitah , mvr sharma
